Education

Cognitive Science (MSc) | University of Jyväskylä | 2021-2023

My strong interest in design and the human side of technology solutions led me to pursue a Master's degree in cognitive science, which focused on human-technology interaction. My studies have focused on human-centered design, visual aesthetics, user research and accessibility. My knowledge about these topics gives me a lot of insight on how to create user-friendly solutions. My studies have taught me a lot about different design perspectives; if needed, I can tell you about the difference between Human-Centered Design and User-Centered Design. I also took courses that discussed digital service design, statistics and cognitive neuroscience. My Master's thesis focused on how novelty and typicality affect the aesthetic appeal of different types of websites

Mathematical Information Technology (BSc) | University of Jyväskylä | 2018-2021

I studied mathematical information technology as my major. During my studies I learned programming with different programming languages (C#, Java, JavaScript, React, Python), which gives me a good understanding of web and software development.
